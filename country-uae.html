Traditional Food

🍛

Machboos

A fragrant rice dish with meat and spices — the national dish of the UAE.

🥩

Al Harees

Slow-cooked wheat and meat, traditionally prepared during Ramadan and weddings.

🍯

Luqaimat

Sweet golden dumplings drizzled with date syrup — a beloved Emirati dessert.

☕

Arabic Coffee (Gahwa)

Cultural Etiquette

  • ✔ Dress modestly, especially in public and religious places.
  • ✔ Accept dates and Arabic coffee — refusing is considered impolite.
  • ✔ Use your right hand to eat, greet, and exchange items.
  • ✔ During Ramadan, avoid eating or drinking publicly during daylight hours.
  • ✔ Greet elders first as a sign of respect.
