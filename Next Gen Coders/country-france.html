Cultural Etiquette

  • ✔ Always say "Bonjour" before starting a conversation.
  • ✔ Greet with light kisses on the cheeks among friends.
  • ✔ Dress neatly — appearance matters in France.
  • ✔ Don't rush meals — they are meant to be enjoyed slowly.
  • ✔ Speak softly in public places.
