Traditional Food

🍚

Koshari

Egypt's national dish — rice, lentils, pasta, and crispy onions in tomato sauce.

🥙

Ful Medames

Slow-cooked fava beans with olive oil, lemon, and spices — a classic breakfast.

🥬

Molokhia

A green leafy soup cooked with garlic and coriander, often served with rice.

🫓

Falafel (Ta'meya)

Crispy fried balls made from fava beans and fresh herbs.

Cultural Etiquette

  • ✔ Greet with "As-salamu alaykum" — it means "peace be upon you."
  • ✔ Accept tea or coffee when offered — it's a sign of hospitality.
  • ✔ Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • ✔ Use your right hand for eating and greeting.
  • ✔ Respect elders — they hold a special place in Egyptian culture.

We Are All Connected

From the Nile to the world — Egyptians share the universal love for family, food, and friendship.
