Cultural Etiquette

  • ✔ Receive gifts and business cards with both hands.
  • ✔ Don't stick chopsticks upright in rice.
  • ✔ Respect elders — they are seated and served first.
  • ✔ Tea is offered as a sign of welcome and gratitude.
  • ✔ Saying "no" directly is often avoided — politeness matters.
