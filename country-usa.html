Cultural Etiquette

  • ✔ Tipping is expected at restaurants (usually 15–20%).
  • ✔ Smile and make eye contact when greeting someone.
  • ✔ Personal space is valued — keep an arm's length when talking.
  • ✔ Be punctual — arriving on time is a sign of respect.
  • ✔ Casual friendliness with strangers is normal and welcomed.
