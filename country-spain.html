Cultural Etiquette

  • ✔ Greet friends with two kisses on the cheeks.
  • ✔ Dinner is late — usually after 9 PM.
  • ✔ Don't be surprised by loud, lively conversations.
  • ✔ Take a siesta in the afternoon when shops close.
  • ✔ Family and friends always come before work.
